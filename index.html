Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How does the XP system work?
A: You earn 1 XP for every step you take. Level up every 5,000 XP to unlock new achievements and features.
Q: Is my health data safe?
A: Absolutely! All your health data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.
Q: Do I need an Apple Watch?
A: No! Step Matrix works perfectly with just your iPhone using the built-in motion sensors.
Q: Can I export my data?
A: Yes! Your data integrates with Apple Health, so you can export it anytime through the Health app.
Q: What happens if I miss a day?
A: Your streak resets, but your total XP and achievements remain. The cyberpunk universe is forgiving!
